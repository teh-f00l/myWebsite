I'm Matilda. I study, imagine, and implement systems at the intersection of art, technology, and community organizing for an equal and equitable world. Preferably in soldarity with th people around me.

About me

Tech

I started programming after I felt I couldn't draw the way I wanted to,
I continued because I realized it was magic like drawing, and made math magic again too
I hesitated when I saw the damage reckless tech-entrepreneurs were doing to the world around me,
I pushed forward hoping to understand them better, so we may all be free,
I persist in pursuit of bigger puzzles and joys through connecting with others,
I pursue distributed systems, security, and analytics so there need be no "others"

Organizing

Given all the problems technologists have "come up with" to solve, as well as exacerbating existing ones, along with the indifference to injustice I found in the art world, I felt I needed to have a direct hand in activism. I found the direct relationship building that comes with being a community organizer to be some of the most rewarding work I have ever done, along with the hardest. This is where I remember where I came from and where I want to go, because I decide this with others.

Art

My artistic practice is a combination of conceptual speculation and craft based contemplation of the unknowable. If that sounded weird, (it certainly did for me) just know that this is my way of understanding and relating to the world around me. Conceptually my interests are currently problemetizing our shared idea of community, the role of collective agency in projects initiated by individuals, ability/disability driven design paradigms, research and tool creation through art, identity(specifically race, gender, sexuality, class, and disability) and cultural dialectics surrounding them
I do not expect to make a career as an artist so a lot of this is just for me and anyone interested, as my practice always has been.
Also Love, Justice, Liberation, and Magic ;)

Some projects

Real Trump news aggregator

This lovely lil program scrapes images from the latest New York Times articles mentioning Donald Trump, dumps them on the user's computer, and creates a new image averaging with the "averaged out" face and saves it. Exhibited at the NYC Media Lab Fake News Horror Show

Conspiracy-Generator

This program scrapes wikipedia for conspiracy theories and generates new ones using Markov Decision Processes

Deep State

A game where players must either collaborate or conspire against each other depending on hidden directives from their leader, with one being a labelled a revolutionary, and the other being a loyalist, always keeping favor with their dictator while sharing eachother's fate, written in Arduino code and deployed on Move38's Blink's Platform
